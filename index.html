WHAT IS VOICEMESSAGES

The ultimate goal of voicemessages is to collect the highest number of Internet users’ voice messages in order to analyze their vocabulary and language level. On voicemessages it is possible, for anyone in completely anonymously way, to record a voice message (maximum 10 seconds) and contribute to the experiment. To encourage people to record theirown voice, users can also listen audio files recordered fron other users in a kind of immense «voice message machine». Once a sufficient number of audio recordings are accumulated, they will be analyzed by an artificial intelligence software, called SyntaxAI, capable of evaluating the lexical level. The AI algorithm, thanks to an inferential engine, Machine Learning and an highly developed linguistic database, is ableto verify if the linguistic content in the audio follows the lexical-grammatical rules of common speech. To best simulate common speech it was decided to leave anonymity for audio recordings. Anonymity also allows users to express theirselfwith a higher level of sincerity. In order to mitigate any illegal audio content, or not in line with the rules of the experiment, all audio files are analyzed by a BOT software capable of identifying illicit content. Any file reported as illicitby any user is automatically deleted by the BOT. Every recorded message, once processed by SyntaxAI software, isdeleted from the platform.
